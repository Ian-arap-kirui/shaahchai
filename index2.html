FAQs

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team at info@shaahchai.com

  • What types of tea do you export?

    We specialize in Kenyan black, purple, green, and white teas, as well as herbal infusions like hibiscus and chamomile.

  • What certifications do your teas have?

    Our teas are Rainforest Alliance and Fair Trade certified, with organic options available.

    MOQ starts at 1 pallet (approx. 50kg) for most products, with flexible options for established buyers.

    We implement rigorous quality checks at every stage, from harvesting to packaging, with samples available for approval.
