About us

From Kenya’s lush highlands to global tea lovers—Shaah Chai Trading Ltd is your trusted source for premium, organic Kenyan teas. We champion a strict no-pesticide policy, ensuring our teas are as pure as the land they grow on. Every blend we craft is a celebration of health, heritage, and sustainability.

A Legacy of Kenyan Tea Excellence

Rooted in the rich soils of Kenya’s tea-growing zones, Shaah Chai Trading Ltd has built a reputation as a reliable exporter of premium-grade Kenyan teas. We deliver consistent bulk volumes and tailor specialty blends from our climate-controlled warehouses in Mombasa.

Our history

  • 2015

    Shaah Chai Trading Ltd is founded with a vision for pure, organic Kenyan tea.

  • 2017

    Opened our first bulk storage facility in Mombasa for international shipments.

  • 2020

    Expanded to over 20 global markets.

  • 2023

    Introduced our signature tea blends for the Middle East and North Africa markets

  • 2025

    Implemented 100% pesticide-free farming compliance across all supplier estates.
